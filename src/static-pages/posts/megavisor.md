# MEGAVISOR

:::article-block role
I was a co-founder and CTO, owning the capture-side R&D around the cloud publishing product.

:::article-block capture-rnd
My work covered the shooting process: inventing the 360-degree product-photography workflow and developing equipment for automated capture of products ranging from phones to motorcycles, including the lighting system and practical production technology. I wrote the technical specification for the controller software; a contracted specialist implemented the first Arduino version.

:::article-block gate9-production
The capture studio inside MEGAVISOR was called Gate9. I organized on-site photo-360 shoots at large customer warehouses and retail stores, found and coordinated photographers and retouchers for image-sequence processing, and turned the capture setup into a field workflow that could run with real inventory and client constraints.

:::article-block photopizza-origin
PhotoPizza was born inside this work: I invented the motorized turntable for MEGAVISOR's affordable, repeatable 360-degree product capture process. After MEGAVISOR, I began developing the equipment-control software myself in JavaScript/Espruino and continued the line independently as an open-source hardware and software project.

:::article-block platform-context
MEGAVISOR belongs in the product-platform line because the hardware and shooting process served a larger portal: cloud publishing, client-facing interactive media, and production operations.

:::article-block player-concept
I contributed product ideas for the MEGAVISOR player, which could present photo-360 sequences, spherical panoramas, and virtual tours and move between those content types — for example, opening the interactive photo-360 of an object from the panorama where it appeared.

:::article-block media-production
I also produced the related media work and organized its production: product [promos](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6CpdVcjtZoU), a [360 photo-studio promo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ySBWZPHZqsw), a [stop-motion promo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=492LvEkyhbg), advertising videos, and training tutorials.

:::article-block founding-team
The project was founded by me and my brother Alex. Alex focused on the cloud product itself and led the development group building the online platform.
